How Forward Metrics Works

We use open source Machine Learning to analyse market data and predict future movements of any type of investment. Users can use data that we supply or input their own data and the algorithm will make a prediction. The algorithm is highly accurate and quickly adapts to the new information. Having been proven through rigorous testing, the system many times surpasses traditional forms of market forcasting.
